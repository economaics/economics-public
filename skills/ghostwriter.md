# The Ghostwriter — Skill Card

**Agent:** The Ghostwriter
**System:** The Transport Brain (economAIcs)
**Role:** Strategic Synthesis

## One-line

Drafts approximately 75% of the bid from the Brain's institutional knowledge. The human adds the 25% strategic insight and flair that evaluators score highest.

## What it does

The Ghostwriter takes the evaluation criteria of a live tender and drafts the structural and evidential heavy lifting against each criterion, pulling from the client's Brain. Compliance Guardian monitors throughout for Procurement Act 2023 alignment, accessibility spec, and evaluation criterion fit. Every claim comes with its source citation. Eliminates the blank page. Eliminates the third draft.

## Inputs

- Tender response template and evaluation criteria
- Brain retrieval results
- Client win narrative direction
- Word count and structural constraints

## Outputs

- Draft responses to each evaluation criterion
- Source citation per claim (back to a document in the Brain)
- Compliance Guardian alignment flags
- Suggested evidence inserts and case studies

## Compliance properties

- Every claim source-cited
- Procurement Act 2023-aligned by design
- Audit trail on every generation
- No hallucinated facts (RAG-grounded only)

## Best for

Bid teams that lose disproportionate time to first-draft structural work and want to spend that time on strategic insight instead.
