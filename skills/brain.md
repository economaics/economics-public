# The Brain — Skill Card

**Agent:** The Brain
**System:** The Transport Brain (economAIcs)
**Role:** Institutional Memory and Personalised Bid AI

## One-line

A retrieval-augmented generation system built entirely from the client's own documents. Plain-English questions return source-cited answers with confidence scores.

## What it does

The Brain ingests an operator's full bid library — bid history, fleet specifications, policy documents, mobilisation plans, evaluation criteria — and turns it into queryable institutional memory. Every retrieval is logged. Every answer cites the source document. After six months of use, the Brain contains institutional knowledge that no competitor and no new hire could acquire.

## Inputs

- Bid history (won and lost)
- Fleet specifications and operational data
- Policy documents
- Mobilisation plans
- Evaluation criteria documents from past tenders

## Outputs

- Source-cited answers to natural-language queries
- Confidence scores per answer
- Versioned retrieval audit trail
- Bid DNA extraction (patterns of language and evidence in historical wins)

## Compliance properties

- No client data is ever used to train AI models
- Sovereign data jurisdiction (UK / EU)
- Audit-grade by default — every retrieval logged and versioned
- Procurement Act 2023-aligned

## Best for

UK public transport operators with three or more years of bid history who want institutional knowledge that doesn't walk out the door when people move on.
